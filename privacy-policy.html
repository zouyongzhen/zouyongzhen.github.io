1. Information We Collect

Depending on the feature you use, we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account and profile data: email, password or login credentials, verification code flow, nickname.
  • Gameplay and social data: room participation, game actions, messages, drawings, and related metadata.
  • File transfer data: file name, file size, MIME type, SHA-256 checksum, transfer status and progress, local transfer metadata.
  • Purchase data: in-app product status and transaction results from Google Play Billing.
  • Technical data: app version, device/network state, push token, crash diagnostics, analytics events, and advertising identifier (AD_ID).
  • Preferences: language selection and other local app settings.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate gameplay, room connection, and social interaction features.
  • Support account login/registration and user session management.
  • Enable file transfer and transfer reliability functions.
  • Process in-app purchases and entitlement status.
  • Send service and gameplay notifications (where permitted).
  • Improve app stability, security, anti-abuse controls, and overall product experience.
  • Provide ads and performance measurement where applicable.

3. How Information Is Shared

We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With other participants in your room when required for gameplay or file transfer.
  • With service providers that support app operations, such as Google Play Billing, Firebase, and AdMob.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.
  • In a business transfer context (such as merger or acquisition), subject to applicable law.

4. Permissions and Device Access

The App may request permissions relevant to feature operation. Examples include:

  • Camera: for QR scanning and room join flow in supported scenarios.
  • Notifications: to deliver transfer status and push-related updates.
  • Foreground service / data sync: to support active file transfers.
  • Network and Wi-Fi state: to manage room connectivity and service reliability.
  • Internet access: required for core online functionality.
  • Advertising ID (AD_ID): for ad-related operations where enabled.
  • Legacy compatibility permissions may exist on certain Android versions or modules.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type.

  • Some data is stored locally on your device (for example preferences and transfer records).
  • You can remove local app data through app settings or by uninstalling the App.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can control certain permissions through your device settings.
  • You can stop using the App at any time and uninstall it.
  • You may request account/data-related support through official support channels.

8. Children and Age-Sensitive Content

The App includes social and party gameplay. Certain content categories may be intended for mature users. Parents or guardians should supervise minor users as required by local law.
